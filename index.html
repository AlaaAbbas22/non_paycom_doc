Welcome to the Payroll System For Non-Paycom students!

This is the documentation for Automation of the different sheets regarding Bill.com to help you navigate, understand, pull information, make updates, or get started in the beginnig of each year. The main sheet is here: Approval Sheet of 2023/2024. To get started, you would need the sheet of last year. Instead of building everything from scratch, we clone the sheet of the previous year with the automation scripts and just update the necessary details.
